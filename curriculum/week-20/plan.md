## Capstone
- Problem statement: Build a documented research artifact that uses AI support for one part of the workflow while keeping the core quantitative logic verifiable.
- Data source suggestions: Use price data, simple text headlines, or a synthetic research corpus plus public market data.
- Implementation steps: Define the question, separate AI-assisted and fully verified steps, implement a reproducible notebook, document prompts and checks, and write a concise report.
- Evaluation metrics: Reproducibility, clarity of verification process, predictive or explanatory usefulness, and professionalism of final presentation.
- Common mistakes: Letting AI invent facts, failing to version prompts or assumptions, mixing future information into features, and not keeping a manual validation trail.
