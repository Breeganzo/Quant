# Week 01 Coverage Audit

## What Was Fixed

The original Week 1 daily lesson PDFs were too thin for serious study. They were roughly 226 to 279 words per day, which was not enough for a real 4-hour weekday learning block.

Week 1 has now been rebuilt with:

- a fuller lesson for each day
- an executed notebook for each day
- a separate executed mini-project notebook
- daily interview drills
- weekly interview questions and model answers
- explicit time blocks so the workload matches the schedule

## Important Interpretation

The daily PDF alone is not meant to consume the full 4 hours as pure reading. A serious learning block includes:

- reading and note-taking
- hand-worked practice
- coding the notebook examples
- writing reflections and interview answers aloud

That is the standard used in this audit.

## Remaining Standard For Future Weeks

Weeks 2 to 24 should follow the same pattern:

- richer daily markdown lessons
- executed notebooks
- weekend project or capstone implementation
- interview prep questions and answers
- explicit time-budget alignment
