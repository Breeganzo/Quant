## Minimum Standard For Week 1

- You can compute and explain a simple return without notes.
- You can explain why compounding matters.
- You can compute expected value for a discrete setup.
- You can explain a dot product as a weighted sum.
- You can compute mean and standard deviation on a toy series.
- You can explain buy-side versus sell-side quant work.
