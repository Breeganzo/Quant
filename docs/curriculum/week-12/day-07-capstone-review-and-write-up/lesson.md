## Formula Sheet Drill
- Expected value: E[X] = sum_i p_i x_i
- Variance: Var(X) = E[(X - E[X])^2]
- Covariance and correlation: Cov(X,Y), Corr(X,Y) = Cov(X,Y)/(sigma_X sigma_Y)
- Compounding: W_t = W_0 * product(1 + r_t)
